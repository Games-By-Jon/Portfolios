Privacy Policy

This will cover all my games.

Updated 09-26-2018

This page details our privacy policy regarding our games and website. For games, this document only applies to our mobile games, which currently include the following games: Tap That Kitty, Space Rage(working title), Don't Get Splat.

When browsing our website, no information is collected from you.

What information do we collect?
Games By Jon itself does not collect any personally identifiable information from anybody. Some games may collect anonymous usage statistics & analytics, such as time spent in levels, stars achieved etc. These analytics are used to improve our games, such as fine-tuning the gameplay experience.

What information do our trusted business partners collect?
As our games are primarily ad supported, non-personally identifiable information (for example your device model, or your country/continent level location) may be collected by our trusted business partners so that they can serve the ads you see.

Also, for social gaming features (such as global leaderboards, achievements and score sharing on social networks) our trusted partners may collect information they need to provide the services they offer. This information is non-personally identifiable except for the cases where you explicitly register (or login) with your e-mail address, for example. Our games will not collect personal information without your consent.
Please check the list below, if you wish to have further information about our partners.

Apart from the trusted partners listed above, no information is disclosed to third parties.

Third party links

We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

Your Consent
By using our mobile games, or using our website, you consent to the terms in this privacy policy.

Changes to our Privacy Policy
We may change this Privacy Policy at any time, at our sole discretion. If we decide to change anything in this document, we will update the Privacy Policy modification date below.
