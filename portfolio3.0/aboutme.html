About Me

Hello I am Jonathan Thompson, as you can no doubt guess I am a video game developer and enthusiast. I love all things to do with games and in the fall of 2014 I took the plunge and start studying at triOS college for video game design and development. The passion for building games started while playing the first Call of Duty, where i started to learn to build maps and loved seeing people play on our server and enjoying something I had created. Finally in the spring of 2014 decided it was a time for a change and choose to do something I had a great passion for and as soon as I started I knew then what I wanted to do for a career. I absolutely fell in love with programming and building games from scratch. After 2 years in college I graduated with honours in the fall of 2016. Since then I have been working on my own projects, in which I have a game released on android devices(working on a port for iOS), as well as a second close to release.

As I stated above I am a game enthusiast, I love going out to conventions or other similar type shows, to check out what other developers are working on, or what kind of new tech is coming out. Being from the Toronto, Canada area I have been to Fan Expo (Toronto), MIGS (Montreal), and Enthusiast Gaming Live Expo (Toronto), maybe I'll see you there one day. Always dreamed of going to E3 or PAX East.
